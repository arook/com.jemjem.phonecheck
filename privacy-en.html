PhoneCheck iOS Test App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2025

1. Introduction

The PhoneCheck iOS Test App (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is a device diagnostic tool developed by JemJem. This Privacy Policy aims to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. By using this App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Types of Information Collected

The App collects the following types of information during device diagnostics:

  • Device Hardware Information: Device model, serial number, battery health status, storage capacity
  • Sensor Data: Test results from accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, barometer, proximity sensor
  • Connectivity Status: Availability and performance data for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, cellular network, GPS, NFC
  • Audio/Video Test Data: Test results for microphone, speaker, camera functionality
  • Display and Touch Data: Screen pixel test results, multi-touch test data
  • Biometric Test Data: Face ID/Touch ID functionality verification results (biometric templates are NOT collected)

3. How Information is Used

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Performing device hardware diagnostic tests
  • Generating diagnostic reports for technical analysis
  • Sending test results to the connected PhoneCheck desktop application
  • Improving app performance and diagnostic accuracy

4. Data Storage and Retention

All diagnostic data:

  • Is temporarily stored locally on the device and can be deleted after testing
  • Can be optionally sent to the connected PhoneCheck desktop application for further analysis
  • Is NOT stored or retained on our servers
  • Does NOT contain Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your diagnostic data with third parties, except when:

  • You explicitly choose to send data to the connected PhoneCheck desktop application
  • Required by law or legitimate request from government agencies
  • Necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety

6. User Rights

Under applicable laws, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your data
  • Restriction or objection to data processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@jemjem.com.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the App or via email. Continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@jemjem.com

We will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
